Toxicity Info

How dangerous is chocolate for cats?

Chocolate contains theobromine, which cats metabolize much slower than humans. The risk depends on the type of chocolate and how much your cat consumed relative to their body weight.

Chocolate type vs. risk level
White chocolateLow concernMonitor at home
Milk chocolateModerateMonitor closely
Dark chocolateHigh riskCall your vet
Baking chocolateEmergencyCall your vet now
Risk increases with amount consumed and decreases with your cat's body weight.

Seeing any of these? Call your vet now.

If your cat shows symptoms, or you're unsure how much chocolate was consumed, contact your veterinarian or an emergency animal poison hotline immediately. Most cats recover fully with prompt attention.
